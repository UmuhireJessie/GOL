Planting Trees competition


We are a team of 5 students working together to solve climate change crisis in Africa starting with East African countries, using technology. We came up with the "Planting Trees Competition" that aims to encourage people to plant trees. Anybody willing to plant a tree can take part in the competition. They simply plant a tree, take a picture of the tree and upload the image. The person who has planted the most number of trees in a month, stand a chance to win a smartphone.
